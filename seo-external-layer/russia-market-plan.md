Updated: 2026-06-20

## Rules

- Do not mass-submit identical text everywhere.
- Use the RU landing for Russian platforms.
- Use the press kit only as source/facts page.
- Do not use English pages, international launch platforms or dollar-focused positioning in the current sprint.
- Position the product as a web service, not as a Telegram bot.
- Avoid guaranteed-views claims.
- Prefer product demos, creator workflow, cost/time saving and content pipeline angles.
