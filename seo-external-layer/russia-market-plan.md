# Russia-First External SEO Plan

Updated: 2026-05-23

Goal: increase Russian organic traffic by building topical trust, citations, first backlinks and referral traffic around the Russian-language landing, guides and product pages.

## Priority Order

1. Submit the product to Russian SaaS and AI directories:
   - Startpack
   - AIList.ru
   - Нейрокаталог
   - Neuro-x

2. Publish founder/case content on Russian platforms:
   - VC.ru: business/product story with demo and honest metrics.
   - Habr: technical article about the video generation pipeline, not a promotional post.

3. Build targeted community mentions:
   - Telegram AI/SMM/YouTube creator channels.
   - VK SMM and creator communities.
   - Dzen articles based on existing RU guides.

4. Pitch marketing/startup media after first proof points:
   - Cossa
   - Sostav
   - AdIndex
   - RB.ru

- Referral sessions from each placement.
- Russian search impressions and CTR in Search Console/Yandex Webmaster.

- Do not mass-submit identical text everywhere.

- Avoid guaranteed-views claims.
- Prefer product demos, creator workflow, cost/time saving and content pipeline angles.
