FAQ

Do faceless Shorts perform worse?

Not automatically. Performance depends more on the first seconds, clarity, pacing and payoff than on whether a face is visible.

What format should I start with?

Start with a mini-guide, checklist, comparison or before-after format because progress is easy to understand.

Can I make a whole faceless channel this way?

Yes, if you create a repeatable content system and keep improving formats from retention data.
