Data Deletion

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Users may request deletion of their data related to the use of AdShorts AI.

To request data deletion, send an email to: support@adshortsai.com

Please include in your email:

  • the email address linked to your account (if any);
  • Telegram user ID and/or YouTube channel ID (if YouTube was connected);
  • subject line: Data Deletion Request - AdShorts AI.

Data deletion requests are processed within up to 30 calendar days, unless longer retention is required by law.

If you connected YouTube via Google OAuth, you can also revoke app access at: myaccount.google.com/permissions.

For details on data handling, see our Privacy Policy.
