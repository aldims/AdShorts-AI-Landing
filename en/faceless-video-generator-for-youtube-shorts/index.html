FAQ

What makes a faceless video work?

A specific topic, clear narration, readable subtitles and visuals that move the idea forward.

Can I use photos?

Yes. Photos work best when they are paced like a story or checklist, not left as a slow slideshow.

Should every faceless Short have voiceover?

Usually yes, because voiceover gives pace and context while captions support silent viewing.
